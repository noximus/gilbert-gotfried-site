Mr. Tom Abraham
Night Court
Warner Bros. Inc.

Dear Mr. Abraham:

Thank you for your letter. I am sorry Night Court will not be taping during our visit in late October.

The first show of the season aired here this week and I was horrified to see the so- called comic Gilbert Gottfried in the cast. What on earth is funny about screaming at everyone? I certainly won't watch the second part of this 2-parter without him being removed.

Surely he did enough disgusting material on the recent awards show to justify him being banned from TV for life. He ruined your excellent show. I just don't understand how anyone could cast such a revolting performer in such a great show.

Hopefully he will be out by the third show of the season. Then I may watch again.

Sincerely,

Charles Foster
